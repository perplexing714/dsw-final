MSpaint Arts

"Lucy"

This piece combines a tennis court setting with an MSpaint verison of Lucy Kronberg. The realistic face, with its wide-eyed gaze and heart-shaped earrings, floats incongruously in the scene, the structured world of sports with the absurdity of this art. This clash between the real and surreal creates a humorous and unexpected visual experience.

Made by Tyler Satterberg
