"The One Who Knocks"

This piece combines pop culture references and surreal humor, blending the imagery of a Gorgeist with Walter White. Tyler has employed minimalist digital techniques to convey a powerful message. The phrase "I am the one who knocks" coming from "Waltergeist" creates a parody of intensity and absurdity. This piece invites the viewer to reflect on the intersection of cultural phenomena, identity, and the power of visual language in modern memes.

Made by Tyler Satterberg
