"Lobster Telephone"

Lobster Telephone is a surrealist piece that combines a lobster and a vintage telephone, with the lobster's claws acting as the receiver. This unexpected pairing challenges conventional perceptions, blending the natural with the artificial and inviting reflection on communication and the absurd.

Made by Edward James and Salvador Dali
